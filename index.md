Welcome to **Finance Signal Lab**: My corner of the internet and a personal sample space where I break down the noise of modern markets and turn it into something clearer, sharper, and more honest.

This blog is where I share my evolving views on finance, the macro environment, and the signals shaping markets today, all through the lens of an engineer transitioning into quantitative finance. I write openly about what I’m learning, what I’m questioning, and how I interpret the financial landscape, from quant intuition to risk frameworks to the psychology behind decision‑making. Much of this thinking is grounded in the foundations laid by the original Intelligent Investor, Benjamin Graham.
